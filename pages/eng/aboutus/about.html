About Light Effect Edu

Light Effect educational is an educational consulting firm founded in New York City. Its tutors are all from world-renowned universities such as Harvard, New York University, Stanford and Columbia, and have worked for major educational training institutions, study abroad application centers, top international companies and international organizations, etc. They have a deep understanding of study abroad applications.

Light Effect Education Education also hopes that our students will use this attitude to face the challenges on the way to study abroad and discover their potential.
