Services

How does it Work?

  • School Application Program Consultation and Planning
    Our School Application Program Consultation and Planning service provides students with one-on-one assigned mentors. The mentors will provide comprehensive guidance and advice based on the student's background, GPA, GRE scores, research projects, interests, and other relevant materials. The main objective of the process is to help students gain a clearer understanding of what they should do in the future and what programs they should apply to. The mentors will guide students on what preparations are needed during the application process and provide timeline planning suggestions. They will also answer any questions regarding school and program selection. Additionally, if students have any further questions regarding school selection, they can continue to ask the mentors through WeChat.

  • Standardized Test Study Guidance
    ·Taught by former New Oriental trainers and teachers from prestigious universities in the United States, all holding official Cambridge University British Council training certificates. Course options include: GRE Writing Course (5 sessions) | TOEFL Speaking Course (10 sessions) | IELTS Speaking Course (10 sessions) | Academic Oral Expression/Writing Course (10 sessions)）

  • Personal Statement Services and Guidance
    Students will be assigned a mentor specializing in the direction the student wishes to apply to. The mentor will schedule one-on-one voice conversations with the student to jointly explore how to effectively incorporate the student's various materials (research experiences, internships, courses taken, education experiences, personal experiences, etc.) into a well-structured, clear, and logically written personal statement. The discussion will cover the overall structure of the personal statement, the content needed in different paragraphs, and where specific materials should be placed within the statement. After the guidance on the personal statement, students will have a clearer understanding of the overall structure and a more defined starting point. If there are any further questions regarding the personal statement, students can continue to ask the mentor. After completing the personal statement, the mentor will review its structure and ensure it aligns with the intended direction. If there are any issues with the structure or content, the mentor will continue to guide the student in making revisions. By the end of the service, students will have a well-structured and framed personal statement, ready for the next step of revisions.

To showcase the value of the top mentors in the Synthesis Education Elite Team, we have introduced the meticulously crafted Diamond Mentor Accompaniment Program. To ensure that we consistently exceed the average market service standards, we have selected the strongest individuals in our team. They will not only personally guide you through the application process and answer all your application-related questions but also provide ongoing support even after the application season. Whether it's future course selection, career planning, job applications, or interviews, they will provide you with invaluable personal experiences and may even offer internal referral opportunities. Furthermore, the benefits you gain go far beyond these services and assistance.
