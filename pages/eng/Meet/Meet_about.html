University Application Mentorship Program

In the journey of pursuing excellence, having a unique mentor by your side will be the key to your success. Now, we have created a special mentorship program for high school and college students to help you unlock your unlimited potential.

Our mentorship team consists of experienced teachers who graduated from top universities and understand your needs and goals. Through our carefully designed mentorship program, we provide the following support and guidance:

Personalized Consultation: We will schedule appointments based on your availability for one-on-one voice conversations. During this process, we will learn about your background and discuss your experiences, motivations, interests, and achievements. We will provide thought-provoking questions to help you uncover your inner strengths and uniqueness.

Essay Guidance: We will guide you in brainstorming and writing personal statements, application essays, and resumes. We will work with you to ensure that your essays and resumes accurately showcase your personality, growth, and talents. We will provide specific advice and editing suggestions to help you create captivating and well-crafted pieces.

Application Planning: We will work together to develop an application strategy that helps you choose the right universities, majors, and academic plans. We will provide professional advice to help you make informed decisions among numerous options. We will also answer your questions about the application process, course selection, career planning, and more.

Ongoing Support: We will stay in touch with you even after the application process is completed, providing long-term support and guidance. We will share insights on career development, academic advice, and practical tips to help you succeed in university life and your future career path.

We understand that each student is unique, so we tailor the mentorship program to your needs and goals, ensuring that you receive personalized and attentive service. With our mentorship team, you will embrace more opportunities, explore your potential, and become the best version of yourself.

About

Light Effect is an educational consulting company founded in New York. Our team of mentors hails from renowned institutions such as Harvard, New York University, Stanford, Columbia University, and other prestigious universities worldwide. They have previously worked at prominent educational training institutions, study abroad application centers, top international companies, and international organizations, providing them with a deep understanding of the study abroad application process..
