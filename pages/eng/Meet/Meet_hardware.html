Duet PCS MT202

The Duet PCS is a USB desktop speakerphone. It has a built-in microphone and speaker, paired with advanced echo cancellation and noise suppression technologies, making it a very powerful yet portable tabletop VoIP video conferencing solution.

Duet EXE MT202

The Duet Executive has same funcions as The Duet PCS + Daisy Chain. It has a built-in microphone and speaker, paired with advanced echo cancellation and noise suppression technologies, making it a very powerful yet portable tabletop VoIP and telephone video conferencing solution.

Application
  • Small offices or home offices
  • Personal home or dorm room conferencing
  • Conferencing on the go
  • Training rooms or class rooms (when daisy chained)
  • Small to medium conference rooms (when daisy chaining)

Features
  • High quality microphone for large pick-up range
  • Full duplex communication
  • Echo cancelling
  • Noise suppression
  • Automatic gain control
  • Plug and play
  • External loudspeaker connector
  • Limitless daisy chaining capability
  • Durable aluminum casing
  • LED lit control buttons

Jabra SPEAK 510

Turn any conversation into a productive conference call in seconds, with the Jabra Speak 510. An intuitive speakerphone that connects via USB and Bluetooth with crystal clear sound. All the conference call capabilities without the complexity.

Features
  • Answer, end, reject and mute calls.
  • Connect to PC via USB or Jabra Link 360.
  • Connect wirelessly to mobile device via Bluetooth.
  • Portable.
  • Omni-directional microphone.
  • Wideband audio for exceptional sound quality.
  • LED, audio indicators and voice guidance.
